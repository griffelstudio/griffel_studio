Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2020

This Privacy Policy explains how personal data is collected, used and stored in relation to applications provided by Griffel Studio.

Do we collect personal information?

All add-ins (the App) published by Griffel Studio in Autodesk App Store follow the next statements:

  1. The App collects model information as it is necessary for the App to perform its intended functions

  2. The App may access User ID of the user to verify the licence via Autodesk Entitlement API and also get access to the camera, but we do not collect any information about the users of the application

Data described in clauses 1 and 2 is not shared with any third parties.

Data described in clauses 1 and 2 is not stored anywhere. It is deleted once the App exits.

If the user does not agree with any clause of the policy the user should not use the App.

Will This Privacy Policy Change?

We may change this Privacy Policy. The updated Privacy Policy will be available here. We will show the date the Privacy Policy was last updated to help you know when we change it. If we make material changes to our Privacy Policy, we will endeavor to provide you with notice before such changes take effect, such as through prominent notice on our website or services or by email.
