WMR Experience

Preview your project in Windows Mixed Reality immersive headset in one click!

Download on the AUTODESK APP STORE

Run WMR Experience command and the add-in will make all necessary preparations for Mixed Reality Portal. Find your project title in the Places picker to walk it in a virtual reality headset. Griffel Studio's Windows Mixed Reality Experience is the fastest and simplest way to take the first look at your project in real scale.


* you must have one of the Windows Mixed Reality headsets set up and running on your PC.
** WMR Experience command is working only on Windows 10 version 1803 and later.

How does it work?

1. Launch the add-in.

Click 'WMR Experience' button and let the preparation finish.

2. Mixed Reality Portal

Start 'Mixed Reality Portal' Windows application.

3. Places Picker

Find your project in the 'Places Picker'.
